How to Play:
The bottom card set is yours.
The top card set is the CPU's.
Click on the playing deck to slap.
Click on your deck to play.

Rules:
Slap on 10s.
Slap on two cards summing to 10.
Slap on a King/Queen set.
Slap on doubles.
Slap on sandwiches.

Difficulty:

Normal
